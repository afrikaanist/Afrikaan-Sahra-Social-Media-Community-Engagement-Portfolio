About Me

I am a social media strategist and community advocate with a passion for creating inclusive digital experiences. With a degree in Computer Science and a Business Specialization from Seattle University, I blend technical expertise with storytelling and data‑driven strategies to engage diverse audiences. My journey as a Somali refugee fuels my commitment to equity and social justice, and my work spans community‑led initiatives, voter engagement campaigns and business development support. I have led social media campaigns, built digital platforms and collaborated across teams to deliver creative, impactful projects.

Projects

Tech4Refugees Platform (2018 – Present)

Founded and developed a platform to enhance social service access for immigrants and refugees in tech‑heavy cities. Designed a user‑friendly multilingual interface to ensure accessibility, and collaborated with community partners to improve data integrity and engagement. Managed social media channels and online campaigns to increase platform adoption and awareness.

Voter Registration Drives (2018 – 2020)

As Coordinator at the Washington Bus Education Fund, registered over 700 new voters and organized drives for more than 100 students. Developed and shared social media content to mobilize young voters and promote civic participation.

Voting Awareness Curriculum (2018 – 2020)

Facilitated programming with Mother Africa, creating a curriculum for voting awareness tailored to King County’s African communities. Engaged local students in hands‑on state election programs and used social media to drive turnout.

Civility Pledge Program (March 2020 – March 2021)

Co‑created and led the Youth COVID‑19 Civility Pledge Campaign after witnessing community members not taking the pandemic seriously. Worked with fellow Alfie Scholars to design an interactive website where young adults could sign a pledge to wear masks, maintain 6‑foot distancing, learn about vaccines and encourage peers to act responsibly. Built partnerships with youth‑affinity groups and emphasised civility, collective responsibility and unity in all communications.

Innovation & Entrepreneurship Center (Sept 2021 – Sept 2022)

As Client Account Manager for the Albers School of Business and Economics, facilitated growth and resource provision for underserved businesses in Seattle’s Central Area. Enhanced business growth through networking, technical assistance and digital marketing guidance.

Financial Analysis & Communication (June 2022 – Jan 2024)

Served as a Financial Analyst at BlackRock, enhancing investor relations through clear communication and a centralized platform. Coordinated between investment teams and client account managers and leveraged data insights to support marketing strategy.

Selected Work Samples

Below are examples of my social media work. Each sample includes a description of the project, my role and a link to view the content where available. For posts on private platforms, please contact me for access.

More Than a Refugee Campaign

Conceptualised and managed a storytelling campaign for Tech4Refugees that highlighted the talents and aspirations of refugees beyond the label of “refugee.” I produced and curated a series of photos, short‑form videos and carousel posts featuring community members and their stories. The campaign partnered with local organisations and influencers to broaden reach and foster engagement. View on Facebook

Youth Voices of Kakuma

Collaborated with youth leaders in Kakuma refugee camp to train them in digital storytelling and help them produce content for the Youth Voices of Kakuma initiative. Through workshops and remote mentoring, we created video testimonials and photo narratives that were shared on social media to amplify refugee youth perspectives. View sample

Digital Gap Awareness

Developed social media posts to promote my presentation, “Digital Gap: How COVID‑19 Increased Technology and Internet Access Inequities and Our Ethical Responsibility to Closing This Gap.” The posts summarised key takeaways from the talk, shared statistics on the digital divide and encouraged community discussions on solutions. View sample

COVID‑19 Civility Pledge Campaign

Co‑launched the Youth COVID‑19 Civility Pledge Campaign at Seattle University to encourage young adults to commit to mask‑wearing, social distancing, vaccination and civil responsibility during the pandemic. I helped design an interactive pledge website, created shareable graphics and coordinated with community partners to amplify the message. Featured on the Alfie Scholars blog. Read article

Civic Engagement & Voter Registration

Created multimedia content for voter‑registration and civic‑engagement drives at the Washington Bus Education Fund. The campaign targeted young voters with infographics, explainer videos and testimonials, leading to increased event turnout and registrations. View sample

Tech4Refugees Platform Launch

Led the social media strategy for the launch of Tech4Refugees, a web platform connecting immigrants and refugees to social services. I crafted launch announcements, feature highlights and community testimonials to drive sign‑ups and media interest. View sample
